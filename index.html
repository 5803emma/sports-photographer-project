MH Photography | Welcome!

Have a look at some of the services I offer below. Click on any image or link to view the Gallery

Hurling

From Junior C League games to Championship Inter-County Hurling, Mark is an equal opportunities photographer and believes that every player deserves recognition for their talent and commitment to the sport.

Soccer

Mark has established his reputation within the Clare District Soccer League and can usually be found at Lees Road or Frank Healy Park wandering the sideline and capturing all the action with his trusty Canon 80D. Keep an eye out for him and don't be afraid to say hello!

Landscapes

Passionate about capturing and sharing the beauty of landscapes through photography, Mark uses his keen eye for detail to ensure the highest quality image from the inception of the photograph through to the creation of the print.

Urban & Rural

Mark is equally enthustastic about capturing images of new born lambs on the wild and windswept cliffs of Achill Island as he is taking photos of businesses, features and people in the bustling market town of Ennis.

Drone

Mark has been involved in a number of different aerial photography and video projects for the past 3 years. He has used his drones for construction surveys, marketing videos, and at various events.

Commercial

You need that perfect image for your campaign and you need to find it quickly and easily. MH photography will capture and edit the best images of your product and offer you a great selection to choose from.

Founder of MH Photography in 2017. Mark is rapidly gaining a reputation as a talented and unique sports photographer, with an eye for the right moment and a passion for perfection. A complete natural with people, Mark's photography is now well established within the Clare District Soccer League. His rapport with club players and supporters has endeared him to the sports community.

If you're looking to work with an independent photographer on any type of project, feel free to get in touch with me to discuss your requirements. I would be delighted to hear from you!
