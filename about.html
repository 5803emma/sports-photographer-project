About Mark

A little bit about who I am and what I do !

I first got started on my photography journey taking photos of nature. I still seek out people and features in urban and rural areas that catch my eye and interest. I love to travel to different parts of Ireland and further afield to take photographs of hidden gems, as well as getting new angles on well-known beauty spots and historical landmarks.

I'm always on the look out for new opportunities to get great photos ! Please feel free to get in touch and let me know if you would like for me to take photos at any of your events, sporting or otherwise.
